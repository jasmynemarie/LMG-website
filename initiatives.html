Current Initiatives

Introducing: Read. Think. Solve., an early literacy and critical thinking tool designed to close education gaps and encourage children to fall in love with reading through imagination, problem-solving and play.

Each kit includes a full-color book featuring an inspiring and engaging story; a puzzle to strenghten and sharpen early math, shape recognition and problem-solving skills; and a companion guide to encourage parents and caregivers to join in and track their reader's progress.

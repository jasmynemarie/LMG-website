LMG Snapshot

Lineage Media Group uses media as a tool to educate, empower, and preserve. We serve the descendants of the enslaved by developing media programs, educational initiatives, and public storytelling projects that highlight the Black American experience.

A Comprehensive Petition for Redress

American Freedmen and their descendents have been historically underserved and underrepresented in this country. We propose a petition to redress the harms caused by chattel slavery in the United States. The petition will be used to create equity and sustainability in the Black American community as a means to create and maintain generational wealth.

Featured Program: Read.Solve.Thrive.

Read.Solve.Thrive. is our nationally focused early literacy and critical thinking campaign. Through multimedia puzzle kits, culturally relevant books, and engaging visual content, we are changing the way underserved children experience learning.

When we help a child unlock a love for reading, we unlock their future.
